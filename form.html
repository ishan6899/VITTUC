How to Register

1)Please go to our agenda pages and read the brief to get the best idea about which agenda(s) you want to go for.
2)You can provide two preferences of agendas and countries for each agenda. The allotment will be done according to availability of seats and experience of applicant.
3)For external delegate (non VIT-VELLORE students) registration, one can opt for accomodation (extra charges applicable). The acomodation form is in the external delegate registration page itself.
4)For internal delegate (VIT-VELLORE students) there is no accomodation facility.
5)Please make sure you are providing your current updated contact details.
6)After online registration please pay the registration fees through our online payment link.
